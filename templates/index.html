"Early childhood education is the key to bettterment of society"

We are driving discoveries to address global challenges, educating the next generation of leaders and partenering with communities to create healthier , more sustainable futures for Kigali Rwanda and the whole Africa. Kigali on the rise!

Centre Scholaire de Kabeza is a school that was founded by parents in 2006. The aim was to serve Kabeza community children. The aim was to reduce commute time and also allow children to build a great and loving community. Our programs are designed to augment children's mastery of 5 diffrent areas. Science, language, Math and community-centric. Children will learn 2 languages: French and english and become fluent in them.
